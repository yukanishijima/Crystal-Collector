instruction
  1. You are given a target score and 4 crystals.
  2. Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  4. You win the game when you match your total score to the target score. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target score.
  5. Every time the game starts, a target score and values of each crystal will be changed.

click any crystal to start!
